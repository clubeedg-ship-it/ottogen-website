1. What Are Cookies?

      
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

3. How to Manage Cookies

      
When you first visit our site, a cookie banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies.

      
You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

      
    
        
  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
    • 
        
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
    • 
        
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
    • 
        
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
    • 
      

      
To reset your cookie consent on this site, clear your browser's localStorage for oopuo.com.
