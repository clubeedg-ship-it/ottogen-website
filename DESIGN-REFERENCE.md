# DESIGN-REFERENCE.md — Oopuo Website Redesign Blueprint

## Design Direction
**Designjoy × Athena hybrid** — Swiss-style minimalism with time-leverage copy.
Colors: lighter, pastel palette (replacing current dark theme).

---

## DESIGNJOY.CO — Structure Analysis

### Visual Identity
- **Dark background, white/light text** (we'll invert to light/pastel)
- **Double font system:** Large display serif/grotesque for headings + clean sans-serif for body
- **Swiss-style grid:** Massive whitespace, strict alignment, visual breathing room
- **No hero images** — copy IS the hero. Typography carries everything.
- **Subtle animations:** Fade-in on scroll, micro-interactions
- **Bold color accents** on CTAs only (one primary color)

### Page Structure (Homepage)
1. **Hero** — One headline. One subtitle. One CTA button. Nothing else.
   - H1: "Design subscriptions for everyone"
   - Sub: "Pause or cancel anytime."
   - CTA: "Start today"
   - *Zero visual clutter. The headline IS the page.*

2. **Social Proof Badge** — Immediately below hero
   - "One subscription to rule them all."
   - Brief origin story (2-3 sentences max)

3. **Feature Grid** — 6 cards, icon + one-liner each
   - "Easily manage your design queue with a Trello board."
   - "No surprises here! Pay the same fixed price each month."
   - "Get your design one at a time in just a couple days on average."
   - "Senior design quality at your fingertips, whenever you need it."
   - "Scale up or down as needed, and pause or cancel at anytime."
   - "Every design is made especially for you and is 100% yours."
   - *Each card: one benefit, one sentence. That's it.*

4. **Testimonials** — 2 quotes, names + titles
   - Kevin O'Leary (Shark Tank)
   - *Authority = trust*

5. **Flexibility Callouts** — 2 micro-sections
   - "Temporarily pause your subscription anytime, no sweat."
   - "Not loving it after a week? Get 75% back, no questions asked."

6. **FAQ** — Accordion, ~14 questions

7. **Footer** — Minimal

### Copy Principles (Designjoy)
- **Maximum 1 sentence per benefit**
- **No paragraphs on the homepage** — every block is a one-liner
- **Conversational tone** — "no sweat", "no questions asked"
- **Zero jargon** — no "leverage", no "synergy", no "solutions"
- **The CTA is always the same:** "Start today" or "See plans"

## ATHENA.COM — Structure Analysis

### Visual Identity
- **Light/cream background** with dark text
- **Serif + sans-serif** font pairing (elegant, premium feel)
- **Generous whitespace** — similar Swiss grid
- **Product screenshots/mockups** interspersed with copy
- **Muted, warm color palette** — premium feel

### Page Structure (Homepage)
1. **Hero** — Outcome-driven headline
   - H1: "The relief is immediate. The ROI is real."
   - Sub: "Athena shows you what to delegate, how to delegate, and pairs you with an Executive Assistant who pays you back in time and impact."
   - CTA: "Get matched with an EA"
   - *Sells the FEELING (relief) and the RESULT (ROI), not the feature*

2. **Positioning Block** — "An evolution in support"
   - "Traditional assistants focus on a limited administrative scope tied to the office. Athena EAs offer daily strategic partnership across your personal and professional worlds."
   - *Old way vs. new way*

3. **Services Grid** — 4 blocks
   - Executive Assistant, EA Management, AI Technology, Enterprise Security
   - Each: title + 2-sentence description

4. **Capabilities Showcase** — "Expect more from an Executive Assistant"
   - 3 category columns: Home & Travel, Well-being & Balance, Execution & Logistics
   - Each: 4 bullet points of specific tasks
   - *Specificity sells. Not "we help you" but "curate gift ideas and book reservations"*

5. **AI Integration** — "AI-powered, human-led support"
   - Short paragraph positioning tech advantage

6. **Coaching Upsell** — "Go further with Executive Coaching"

7. **Social Proof** — Member profile card mockup

8. **Case Studies** — "Art of Delegation" series links

9. **CTA** — "Hire an elite EA"

### Copy Principles (Athena)
- **Lead with the outcome/feeling** ("The relief is immediate")
- **Specificity over vagueness** — list exact tasks, not categories
- **Old way vs. new way** framing
- **Time is the currency** — everything is framed as getting time back
- **Premium positioning** — "top 1%", "world-class", "elite"

## OOPUO SYNTHESIS — The Hybrid Blueprint

### Design System
- **Light/pastel background** (cream, soft white, or light blue-gray)
- **Double font:** Space Grotesk (bold headings) + Inter (body) — already in use, just needs bolder sizing
- **Swiss grid:** Massive whitespace, strict left-alignment for copy blocks
- **Accent color:** Single pastel accent (soft blue, mint, or lavender) for CTAs
- **No stock photos** — use geometric shapes, subtle gradients, or tool icons
- **Typography-first:** Headlines should be 4-6rem, bold, dominating the viewport

### Copy Framework (Designjoy structure × Athena tone)
1. **Hero:** One punchy headline (Athena's outcome-driven style) + one subtitle + one CTA
2. **Positioning:** Old way vs. Oopuo way (2-3 sentences max)
3. **Feature Grid:** 6 one-liner benefit cards (Designjoy's lean format)
4. **Specificity Block:** Exact AI deliverables listed as bullets (Athena's specificity)
5. **Pricing:** Clean, 2-column card layout (Designjoy's simplicity)
6. **Testimonials/Social Proof**
7. **FAQ**
8. **CTA**

### Copy Rules for Oopuo
1. No sentence longer than 15 words on the homepage
2. No paragraphs — one-liners only in feature sections
3. Lead with the feeling/outcome, not the feature
4. List specific deliverables (WhatsApp bots, invoice automation) not categories
5. "Old way vs. Oopuo way" as the core persuasion framework
6. One CTA per section maximum
7. Conversational but premium — not corporate, not casual

### Color Direction
- Background: #FAFAF8 or similar warm off-white
- Text: #1A1A1A (near-black)
- Accent: Soft blue (#7C9EF5) or mint (#6ECFB0) or lavender (#A78BFA)
- Secondary: Light gray (#E5E5E3) for cards/borders
