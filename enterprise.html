What we believe

      
The principles behind Oopuo

    

    

      

        

        
AI for Everyone

        
AI shouldn't require an engineering team. We package powerful technology into practical, affordable services that any business can use — starting at €300/mo.

      

      

        

        
Privacy as Architecture

        
We're committed to privacy-first principles. Your data stays yours, always.

      

      

        

        
Partnership Over Transactions

        
We don't do one-off projects and disappear. We build long-term relationships with our clients — becoming their trusted digital & AI team for years to come.

      

      

        

        
Competitive Advantage Through Technology

        
Small businesses that adopt AI and modern digital infrastructure gain a real edge. We're here to make sure that advantage is accessible — not just for those who can afford six-figure budgets.
