Meet Otto

Self-taught AI architect. Building infrastructure that puts data ownership back in people's hands.

Otto started Oopuo with a conviction: AI should serve the people who use it, not the companies that sell it. That means private models, local infrastructure, and systems that work without sending your data to someone else's cloud.

From building Chimera — a privacy-preserving AI compute layer — to launching funded startups and delivering enterprise systems, Otto leads a team that bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world business impact.

Our Mission

Make AI private, accessible, and unstoppable.

We believe the best AI infrastructure is the kind you own. No middlemen. No data brokers. No compromises on who controls your intelligence.

Our Journey

From idea to infrastructure

2025

Founded Oopuo
