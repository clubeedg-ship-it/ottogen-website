Founder & CEO

Meet Otto

Self-taught technologist and AI architect. Building the digital & AI team that small businesses deserve but could never afford.

Otto started Oopuo with a simple observation: small businesses are overpaying for fragmented digital services while missing out on AI entirely. He set out to build one team that handles everything — website, email, AI, support, strategy — so business owners can focus on what they do best.

With a background in AI infrastructure, startup building, and enterprise systems, Otto leads Oopuo with a hands-on approach: real problems, practical solutions, no fluff.

Our Mission

Small businesses deserve enterprise-grade digital infrastructure.

We believe every business — no matter the size — should have access to competent digital management and practical AI. One team, one subscription, no confusion.
